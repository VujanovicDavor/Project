About

A shoe is a footwear with a firm base made of leather, wood, rubber or plastic, which is always connected to the upper and which primarily serves to protect the sole of the foot.
Components:
Shoes consist of two main parts: The upper part is called the upper, the lower part is called the bottom. The upper consists of several layers and individual parts glued or sewn together: Inner shaft (lining), intermediate layer (intermediate lining) & outer layer. The outer socket can also have different trimming parts, e.g. a back cap around the heel area to further stabilize and guide the foot. The outer shaft is divided into different areas, the leaf with the tongue at the front and the side quarters at the back.
