Nike Revolution 5

Minimalistic and Small


The Nike Revolution 5 cushions your run with soft foam, making it comfortable to wear. The light knit material surrounds the foot and ensures breathable support and thanks to the minimalist design you are ready for anything.

Breathable hold
The light knitted textile wraps the foot for breathable comfort. The heel and details on the outside are reinforced to ensure support and durability.

Strong damping, light weight
The soft midsole made of foam ensures a smooth, stable running feeling. The structured outer wall reduces the weight of the shoe.

Durable, flexible traction
The rubber outsole enables hard-wearing traction on different surfaces. The special profile enables natural flexibility.

Product Information
  • Soft insole
  • Soft lining
  • Weight: 302g (men's shoe size 42)
  • Heel drop: 10mm (forefoot 14.5mm, heel 24.5mm)
  • Color Shown: Black / Anthracite
