Nike Flex Experience RN 8

FLEXIBLE WEARING COMFORT


The Nike Flex Experience RN 8 offers lightweight comfort that adapts to every step. Soft knit material wraps around your foot, while flex grooves in the outsole ensure adaptive running on every route.

Benefits
  • Flex grooves on the forefoot allow natural flexibility
  • Knitting material adapts to your foot and thus ensures a tight fit.
  • Soft material in the heel ensures an adaptive fit.
  • A seamless coating on the laces improves durability.
  • The hard-wearing, injected midsole ensures a robust running feeling and also serves as an outsole. The result is less rubber and a lower overall weight.
  • Hexagonal flex grooves offer six flexibility points for freedom of movement when running.
  • The textured outsole provides better traction and durability.
  • The rounded heel adapts to the surface for natural movements when rolling.

Product Information
  • Forefoot and heel offset: 6 mm (forefoot 14 mm, heel 20 mm)
  • Color Shown: Black / Cool Gray / Reflect Silver / White
