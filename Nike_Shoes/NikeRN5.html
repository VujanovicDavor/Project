Nike Free RN 5

LESS IS MORE.


The Nike Free RN 5.0 returns to its origins as a running shoe and is ideal for runs up to five kilometers. The revised flex-notch pattern and the modified midsole foam offer more flexibility than the previous model and ensure a barefoot-like feel with every step. The upper material has breathable and elastic mesh with the back of your foot and synthetic leather covers ensure a stable hold.

Benefits
  • The elastic upper material fits like a second skin.
  • The foam with a lower density ensures a feeling of comfort close to the ground.
  • Notches from the heel to the toes allow multi-directional flexibility.
  • The asymmetrical lacing reduces the pressure on the back of the foot.
  • The inner shoe construction with integrated tongue guarantees a tight, seamless fit.
  • The heel area without insert ensures more freedom of movement and a secure hold.
  • The insole ensures a better feeling for the floor.
  • Notches carved into the midsole with a hot knife ensure optimum flexibility with every step.
  • Rubber pods on the forefoot and heel improve traction.

Product Information
  • Weight: approx. 236 g (men's size 10)
  • Forefoot and heel offset: 6 mm (forefoot 14 mm, heel 20 mm)
  • Top-fit
  • Color Shown: Dark Raisin / Black / Aura


Freedom for your feet

The Nike Free RN 5.0 feels like it's part of your foot. Developed for short runs up to 5 kilometers, it supports your entire range of motion with its minimalist, deep profile and ensures that you never feel restricted.

Natural freedom of movement

The Nike Free midsole is 26% more flexible than last year's model and allows you to stretch your midfoot, spread your toes and push yourself off the floor without any perceived interference.


Sits like a second skin

The stretchy upper with an open knit pattern fits snugly around the foot while ensuring breathability. It is reinforced with synthetic suede and offers abrasion protection and durability.


Low design
