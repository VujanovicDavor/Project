Nike Free RN 5

2-TONE SHIFT.


The Nike Downshifter 9 combines a closed mesh on the back, which secures your foot during intensive sprints, with open-mesh mesh in the toe area, which ensures optimal ventilation. Soft foam and flexible grooves ensure a smooth running feeling.

Benefits
  • The open-mesh mesh in the forefoot allows cool air to enter and heat to escape.
  • Closed mesh in the entire midfoot and heel area provides structure and support.
  • The soft, supple foam midsole provides cushioning.
  • The narrow heel looks elegant and feels fast./li>
  • The rubber outsole ensures robust traction.
  • Flex grooves ensure natural movements.
